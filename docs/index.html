Probability of Mr. A's Commute

Equation

P(C|X) = P(X|C) * P(C) / P(X)

Question

Mr. A has 4 ways to commute to work: car, bus, train, and boat.

  • If Mr. A drives himself, there is a 30% chance of being late for work.
  • If Mr. A takes the bus, there is a 50% chance of being late for work.
  • If Mr. A takes the train, there is a 6% chance of being late for work.
  • If Mr. A takes the boat, there is a 10% chance of being late for work.

One day, Mr. A arrives late for work. His boss wants to know:

  • What is the probability that Mr. A drove to work?
  • What is the probability that Mr. A took the train to work?
  • What is the probability that Mr. A took the boat to work?

The boss considers all of Mr. A's commuting options equally likely.

Answer

We will calculate the probability for each commute option using the provided equation.

  • Car

    Equation: (0.3 * (1/4)) / ((0.3 * (1/4)) + (0.5 * (1/4)) + (0.06 * (1/4)) + (0.1 * (1/4)))

    Answer: 0.3125

  • Bus

    Equation: (0.5 * (1/4)) / ((0.3 * (1/4)) + (0.5 * (1/4)) + (0.06 * (1/4)) + (0.1 * (1/4)))

    Answer: 0.5208

  • Train

    Equation: (0.06 * (1/4)) / ((0.3 * (1/4)) + (0.5 * (1/4)) + (0.06 * (1/4)) + (0.1 * (1/4)))

    Answer: 0.0625

  • Boat

    Equation: (0.1 * (1/4)) / ((0.3 * (1/4)) + (0.5 * (1/4)) + (0.06 * (1/4)) + (0.1 * (1/4)))

    Answer: 0.1042
